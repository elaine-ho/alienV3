Name: Jagd Kraing
Age: 6 Billion
Danger Level: 5~Notes: Having evolved in a harsh and frigid land, this planetfs inhabitants are natural born hunters. They honed themselves to be the mightiest army that has ever existed in this galaxy. Getting some assistance from these beasts can be useful, but be careful. Their skills bring a lot of security to the table, but it can endanger the planet as well.
